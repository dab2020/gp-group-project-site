Interviews in locality

Videos

Our group conducted another series of interviews from adolescents only to obtain their perspective regarding the significance of sports as essential recreational activities. Lockdowns and restrictions imposed to curb the spread of COVID-19 drastically affected the outdoor sports of adolescents as they were unable to go to their grounds. This, inversely, affected their mental health, physical health, and sports skills. Now that vaccines for COVID-19 have been introduced worldwide, a slight betterment can be seen.
