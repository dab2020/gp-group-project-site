About Us

We are O Level Students working for our Global Perspectives Team Project. The domain that we have chosen is Sport and Recreation. We will be assessing the change in the trend of recreational activities during COVID-19. We will be spreading awareness about the significance of sports as essential recreational activities and the importance of limiting screen time. We will also be analyzing the difference of perspectives between adolescents and adults regarding the change in the trend of recreational activities during COVID-19. We would like you to support us and join us on this initiative to revive recreation!
