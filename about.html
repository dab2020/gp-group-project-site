About Us

We’re a group of O level students working for our Global Perspectives 2069 Team Project. By our group name “Reviving Recreation”, we mean that we’ll be focusing on spreading awareness amongst people about returning to healthy recreational activities and understanding the severity of online recreational activities’ consequences.

We will be assessing the change in the trend of recreational activities during COVID-19. We will be spreading awareness about the significance of sports as essential recreational activities and the importance of limiting screen time. We will also be analyzing the difference of perspectives between adolescents and adults regarding the change in the trend of recreational activities during COVID-19. We would like you to support us and join us on this initiative to revive recreation!
